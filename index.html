What We Do

Our Craft

We're the paper experts — not design or paint people. Every service we offer is laser-focused on one thing: making your walls extraordinary.

01

Wallpaper Installation

Expert installation of pre-pasted & unpasted wallpapers, ensuring flawless alignment and seamless pattern matching for a perfect finish.

02

Custom Murals

Breathtaking hand-selected murals, weathered-stone finishes, and hand-crafted torn paper products that transform spaces into works of art.

03

Texture & Specialty

Complex pattern work, textured custom wallcoverings, and specialty finishes that demand an expert hand and decades of experience.

04

Consultation

Personalized on-site visits to assess your space, discuss your vision, and provide expert guidance on wallcovering selection and design.

05

Wall Preparation

Professional surface preparation including old wallpaper removal, priming, and wall conditioning to ensure a flawless foundation.

06

Mentoring

Sharing four decades of expertise with the next generation of wallcovering professionals through hands-on training and guidance.

How We Work

Your Journey to
Beautiful Walls

01

Discovery & Consultation

We begin with a conversation about your space, your style, and your aspirations. Whether in-person or virtual, we'll assess your walls and discuss possibilities.

02

Design & Selection

We'll guide you through wallcovering options, provide samples, and help you visualize the transformation. Our expertise ensures the perfect match for your space.

03

Wall Preparation

Great results start with great preparation. We meticulously prepare surfaces — removing old coverings, priming, and conditioning — for a flawless foundation.

04

Expert Installation

With 45+ years of precision, every seam is invisible, every pattern is perfectly aligned. This is where decades of mastery meet your vision.

05

The Reveal

Step back and fall in love with your space. We ensure every detail exceeds expectations before we consider the project complete.
